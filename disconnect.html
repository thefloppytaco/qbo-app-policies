Disconnect

You can revoke this application's access to your QuickBooks Online data at any time, directly from your QuickBooks Online account.

How to disconnect

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online at qbo.intuit.com.
  2. Click the gear icon (⚙) in the top-right corner.
  3. Under Tools, click Manage apps (or go to Apps → My Apps).
  4. Find this application in the list of connected apps.
  5. Click Action → Disconnect, then confirm.

Once disconnected, the application will no longer be able to read or modify any data in your QuickBooks Online company. The operator stores no QuickBooks data outside of QuickBooks Online itself, so no further deletion request is necessary.
