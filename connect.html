Connect or Reconnect

This is an internal QuickBooks Online integration operated by Moshe Baum. The application is a locally-installed tool used to read and update QuickBooks data on behalf of authorized users (the operator and the operator's bookkeeping clients).

To connect

Connections are established through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow during initial setup, performed by the operator. End users (clients) do not need to take action to connect — once you have invited Moshe Baum as an accountant on your QuickBooks Online company, the operator handles the connection on the backend.

To reconnect after a token expires

If the application reports an authorization error, the operator will run the OAuth flow again to issue a fresh refresh token. No action is required on the client's end.
